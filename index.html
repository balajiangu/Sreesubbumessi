About Our Cafe

Welcome to Sree Subbu Mess, where we take pride in serving you the best of South Indian cuisine, with a special focus on our mouth-watering biryani and the beloved idly kadai. Nestled in the heart of the community, our restaurant embodies the essence of traditional cooking, bringing you authentic flavors that tantalize your taste buds. Each dish is meticulously prepared using time-honored recipes, ensuring that every bite transports you to the roots of rich culinary heritage. At Sree Subbu Mess, we believe that good food brings people together.

Our cozy and welcoming atmosphere makes it the perfect place for families and friends to gather, celebrate, and relish sumptuous meals. We source only the highest quality ingredients, prioritizing freshness and sustainability, so you can indulge in delicious meals that are also good for you. Whether youre craving a hearty biryani or the comforting goodness of idly kadai, let us take you on a delightful culinary journey that keeps you coming back for more.
